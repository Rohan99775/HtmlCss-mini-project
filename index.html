AMAZING TEAM WORK WITH PROFESSIONAL PHOTOGRAPHER

Over the last few years working in professional portrait photography I have learnt that the success of my endeavours is not solely down to me. It is all about teamwork: ‘a co-ordinated effort on the part of a group of persons acting together as a team in the interests of a common cause’.So much of the success of portrait photography lies in the expression or body language of the subject. Photographers seem to think that they are responsible for everything – light, composition, mood – which to a large extent they are. But it takes a very willing actor to mask boredom, self-consciousness etc.
